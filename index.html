The problem

Research is fragmented by design.

The tools researchers use were built independently, for different jobs. They don't talk to each other. Your thinking dies in the gaps between them — every single session.

01
You switch between 6 tools just to finish a lit review
Elicit, Zotero, Notion, ChatGPT, Google Docs, your email. Context collapses every time you switch.
02
No tool holds your context session to session
You're a stranger every time you open a new chat. The thinking from yesterday lives nowhere.
03
AI tools hallucinate citations
Plausible-sounding, completely fabricated. Verifying every source yourself defeats the purpose of using AI at all.
04
Prism starts at the writing stage
OpenAI's tool is excellent once you know what to write. The messy pre-writing stage — the hardest part — is still entirely yours.
Our approach

How we're solving it.

We're not guessing at solutions. Each problem has a specific technical approach — here's exactly what we're building and why.

01
Citation grounding, not generation
Every claim in your brief will be anchored to text extracted directly from your uploaded PDFs. If it's not in your papers, it won't appear in your brief.
RAG · Strict source grounding
02
Gap identification from the literature itself
We'll map what your uploaded papers collectively claim — and surface where the claims run out. Research gaps found in the text, not invented by the model.
Semantic clustering · Coverage analysis
03
Persistent project context
Your research question, uploaded papers, and synthesis outputs will be stored per-project. Pick up exactly where you left off — no re-uploading, no re-explaining.
Supabase · Embeddings · Session state
04
Thematic clustering across papers
Instead of summarizing papers one by one, Primer will cluster findings thematically across your entire upload — surfacing debates and contradictions automatically.
Cross-document synthesis
05
Outline generation, not writing
Primer's job ends at the outline. We'll hand you a structured, citation-ready skeleton to take into any writing tool — Prism, Word, LaTeX, whatever you prefer.
Structured output · Export-ready
06
Privacy-first by architecture
Your uploaded PDFs and research data will be processed in isolated environments. We're designing this from day one for researchers with IRB-protected and unpublished data.
Isolated processing · No training on your data
How it will work

Three steps.
One brief.

i
Upload your papers
Drop in up to 50 PDFs. Primer reads everything — methods, findings, citations, limitations. Nothing gets skipped, nothing gets invented.
ii
State your research question
Plain language. No boolean operators, no keyword gymnastics. Primer maps your question against the full literature landscape of your uploaded papers.
iii
Get your research brief
Thematic synthesis, key debates, contradictions, and identified gaps — every claim traced back to its source. Structured and ready to take into any writing tool.
